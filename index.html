Sourya Basu

I am Sourya Basu, currently a Junior Research Fellow at Multi Modal Information Processing Lab, at IIT Kanpur. I received my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering, with a minor in Artificial Intelligence (offered by the department of Computer Science, IIT Kanpur) in June, 2017. I spent the summer of 2016 at Information and Intelligence Group, University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign working with Prof. Lav Varshney on compression of graphical structures. I am intrigued by Information theory, Data compression and Artificial Neural Networks. I wish to explore these areas in depth and am looking for Ph.D. & research positions in the same.
